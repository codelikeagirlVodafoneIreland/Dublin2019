Mountain Gorilla

Weight: Fully-grown male mountain gorillas can weigh up to 180 kg (400 lb). Females weigh half that at about 90 kg. ( 200 lb)




Description: In addition to its size, the mountain gorilla has longer fur than its other relatives. Its long, black fur and large mass help keep it warm in below freezing temperatures in the mountains. Mountain gorillas have thick fur to keep them warm.




Diet: About 67% of their diet is fruit, 17% is leaves, seeds and stems and 3% is termites and caterpillars. This subspecies consumes parts of at least 104 plant species.




Poaching: To this day, poaching continues to jeopardize the gorillas' survival, It is now largely the result of unselective hunting with snares.
